skleia.com

custom size plywood furniture

skleia.com is a brand specialized in crafting plywood furniture and accessories with use of CNC technology. We stand out with unique designs, focus on ergonomic and detail orientated approach. Our killer feature is ability to customize each of our designs very fast with fraction of inch accuracy.
